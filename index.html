  • Ocmulgee Mounds National Historic Park

    Ocmulgee Mounds National Historic Park

    The Ocmulgee Mounds National Historic Park has been the home to various Native American tribes for more than 17,000 years, including the Ochese Creek Nation who consider it to be the place where their ancestors first settled down.
